Birthday Game for Ahona

Game Instructions

- Tap the screen or space button to make the Ahona jump.
- When Ahona eats a flower power-up, it grants her the ability to pass through pipes without losing a life for 6 seconds.


Special Rewards:

  1. If Ahona scores 30 points, she will receive a special reward.
  2. If Ahona scores 50 points, she will receive another special reward.
  3. If Ahona scores 100 points, she will receive yet another special reward.
